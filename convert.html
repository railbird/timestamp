This is a NodeJS Microservice to convert UNIX timestamps into natural date and vise versa.

    1) Enter a UNIX timestamp and it gets converted to a natural date.
    2) Enter a natural date and it gets converted to a UNIX timestamp.
    3) Invalid inputs will result in a null response.

Example Usage:

https://unixtimestampconvert.herokuapp.com/December%2015,%202015
https://unixtimestampconvert.herokuapp.com/1450137600

Example Output

{ "unix": 1450137600, "natural": "December 15, 2015" }
